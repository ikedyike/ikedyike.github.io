DINO FALL:

Dino Fall is a short platformer game I have been working on developing in my free time from the ground up. It is a game where you play as a small dinosaur who is trying to find its way down a mountain. The goal of the game is to scale your way down the side of the cliff, but if you fall too far you have to restart! When you fall too far, you have to restart your decent from the beginning, aka: it gets easier the more you play.

How to properly fall: you want to find the shortest path down.

How not to fall: you do not want to fall from too high up.
